## What is Sylven OS?

Sylven OS is not just another GitHub organization.

It is a community — built from the ground up for developers who believe that the best software is written together, that knowledge shared is knowledge multiplied, and that every programmer deserves a place to grow.

We are a **technology-agnostic** open-source ecosystem. Python, Rust, Go, JavaScript, C++, WASM — all languages, all stacks, all backgrounds are welcome here. What unites us is not a single tool, but a shared commitment to learning, building, and educating.

> *"They say the best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."*
> Today, we are planting the first seed.

---

## Our Three Pillars

### 🌱 To Learn
No one is ever finished learning. Sylven OS is a space for experimentation, where mistakes are celebrated as steps forward. Whether you're writing your first `Hello World` or designing distributed systems — this is your laboratory.

### 🔨 To Build
We are creators. We turn abstract ideas into functional software. Every contribution matters — from fixing a typo in the docs to architecting a new project from scratch. Your code has a home here.

### 📖 To Educate
The cycle is only complete when knowledge is shared. Every contributor is a teacher. Every project is a curriculum. We believe in open knowledge, visible processes, and radical transparency.

## Our Community Guidelines

We are building something meaningful here, and that starts with how we treat each other.

- **Be kind.** Everyone was a beginner once.
- **Be constructive.** Feedback builds people up, not down.
- **Be open.** Share what you know. Ask what you don't.
- **Be patient.** Open source runs on goodwill and free time.

Read our full [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) before contributing.
