## 🌍 What is Sylven OS?

> *They say the best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.*
> **Today, we plant the first seed.**

**Sylven OS** is not just another GitHub organization. It is a **movement** — a collaborative open-source ecosystem designed to connect developers, students, and builders from every corner of the world.

We are **technology-agnostic**. Python, Rust, Go, JavaScript, C++, Zig, WASM — every language, every stack, every background is welcome. What unites us is not a single tool, but a shared belief:

- 🧠 That **learning** never stops
- 🔨 That **building** together is better than building alone
- 📖 That **sharing knowledge** is the most powerful thing a developer can do

## 🤝 How to Contribute

We welcome contributors at every level. Here's how to get started:

```
1. ⭐  Star this organization and follow our repos
2. 💬  Join our Discord — introduce yourself in #introductions
3. 🔍  Browse open issues — look for "good first issue" labels
4. 🍴  Fork a repo, make your changes, open a pull request
5. 💡  Have an idea? Open a GitHub Discussion or suggest it in Discord
```

> 📖 Read the [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](./CONTRIBUTING.md) in any repo before your first PR.
> 🛡️ All contributors must follow our [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
